- Our Mission -

Petinder is app that helps people match themselves up with pets that may never be seen otherwise.
Our website allocates the use of API's to pull pet profiles from local shelters along with other sources.

- About Us -

This is our Project utilizing API's such as the Petfinder API to display Pet Information in a Tinder-Like App, which allows the user to easily browse through companion pets available for adoption.
This team was organized and received guidance from mentors at Northwestern Coding BootCamp in Evanston, Illinois.
